Our Mission

We believe every child deserves a school worth learning in.

Owu College Foundation exists to close the gap between children and a safe, functional classroom. Gifts fund school renovations, scholarships, and community outreach — every naira directed where it changes a life.

Vision

To create safe, inclusive, and inspiring school learning environments where every child has the opportunity to learn, grow, and reach their full potential.

Mission

To mobilize communities, partners, and donors to renovate and equip primary and secondary schools, creating safe and conducive learning environments that empower children and strengthen the foundation of education.

Where We Began

Born From OWUTECH

The Owu College Foundation was established in response to the growing challenges confronting many public schools and underserved communities. Across many communities, children continue to learn in classrooms with deteriorating infrastructure, inadequate furniture, poor sanitation, limited learning resources, and environments that do not support effective teaching and learning.

The founders recognized that improving educational outcomes begins with creating dignified, safe, and conducive learning environments. They also understood that sustainable development requires addressing other social challenges such as poor healthcare access, youth unemployment, poverty, and inadequate community support systems.

The Foundation was therefore created to mobilize resources, inspire partnerships, and implement impactful programmes that improve educational infrastructure, empower young people, strengthen communities, and promote social development.
